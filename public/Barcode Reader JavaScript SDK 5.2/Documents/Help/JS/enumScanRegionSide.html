EnumScanRegionSide Enumeration

Description

An enumeration that represents the scan regions for barcode reading.

Member Description
ESRS_Left The barcode reading will be performed on the left side of the image in %.
ESRS_Top The barcode reading will be performed on the top side of the image in %.
ESRS_Right The barcode reading will be performed on the right side of the image in %.
ESRS_Bottom The barcode reading will be performed on the bottom side of the image in %.

Remarks

By default, the barcode reader will search the whole image. You can speed up the recognition process by specifying a search area.
